chAccAgeInd
Type: string
One of: 01 02 03 04 05
Optional
Length of time that the cardholder has had the account with the 3DS Requestor.

Interpretation of values:

01
No account (guest check-out)
02
Created during this transaction
03
Less than 30 days
04
30−60 days
05
More than 60 days
